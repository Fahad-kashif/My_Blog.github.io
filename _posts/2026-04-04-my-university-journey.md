# 🎓 My University Journey

This post documents my growth and experiences as a Computer Engineering student at UET.

---

## 1. Stepping into University Life

### First Impressions
The campus was huge and intimidating on the first day. I felt nervous but also excited to explore this new environment.

### Finding Guidance
Friendly seniors helped me navigate classrooms, labs, and departments, making my first days manageable.

### Adjusting to Routine
Learning to balance commuting, lectures, and assignments taught early lessons in time management.

## 2. First Experiences with Coding

### Entering the Lab
My first programming lab was exciting and intimidating at the same time.

### Trying Simple Programs
Running small programs for the first time gave me confidence and a sense of achievement.

### Curiosity and Confidence
Small successes encouraged me to explore coding beyond assignments.

### Learning Fundamentals
Loops, conditions, and functions were initially challenging but became manageable with practice.

Debugging failed programs taught patience and persistence.

### Tracking Improvement
Gradual progress in assignments and quizzes boosted technical confidence.

## 4. Programming Skills Development

### Learning C#
I developed object-oriented programming skills using C#, building structured applications and solving problems.

### Python Programming
Python enhanced my scripting, automation, and algorithmic problem-solving abilities.

### Database Skills (MySQL)
I learned database creation, SQL queries, table design, and backend data handling.
